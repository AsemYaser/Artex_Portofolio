About Us

Brand Identity &
Modern Aesthetics

Founded in 2021 in Upper Egypt, Artex Design Studio is a creative hub dedicated to delivering distinctive interior and exterior design solutions.

We specialize in transforming spaces into visually compelling and functional environments, combining aesthetic vision with practical design approaches. Our work reflects a deep understanding of modern trends while respecting the unique identity of each project.

At Artex, we believe that every space tells a story. That’s why we focus on creating designs that not only look exceptional but also enhance the experience of those who use them.

From concept to visualization, we are committed to bringing ideas to life with precision, creativity, and attention to detail.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Shaping inspiring spaces and delivering exceptional design services to our clients across the Middle East.

Our Vision

To become a leading design studio across the Middle East, recognized for delivering innovative and high-quality interior and exterior design solutions. We aim to expand our presence in key markets, especially in Saudi Arabia and the UAE, while building a strong reputation for creativity, precision, and excellence. Our vision is to shape inspiring spaces and become a trusted name in both design and real estate development sectors.

Our Mission

At Artex Design Studio, our mission is to create unique and functional spaces that reflect our clients' identity and vision. We are committed to delivering exceptional design services with attention to detail, modern aesthetics, and practical solutions.
